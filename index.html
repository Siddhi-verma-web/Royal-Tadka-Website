Our Story

A Royal Tradition,
Served with Modern Grace

Born from a family recipe book passed down four generations, Royal Tadka brings the grandeur of Indian royal kitchens — the khansama tradition — into a contemporary dining room. Every dish begins with ingredients sourced fresh each morning, slow-cooked spice blends ground in-house, and recipes that have never left the family.

  • Fresh, authentic ingredients sourced daily
  • Traditional recipes, four generations strong
  • Award-winning, experienced master chefs
  • 100% hygienic, FSSAI-certified kitchen
  • Warm, family-friendly ambience
  • Luxury dining, everyday accessible
